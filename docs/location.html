Bahamas Guide for Applicants for Research and ABS Permits

Step 4 Location

Status: Working Draft

The map allows you to search Google Maps and select one or more research locations. Click save for each individual location where you intend to conduct research and then add a new location.

We hope to offer bounding box markers in future.

Note that for applications for marine research you are required to provide a document containing:

  1. The precise locations for the research
  2. A cruise plan
