Bahamas Guide for Applicants for Research and ABS Permits

Registration

Status: Working Draft

Applicants will need to use their ORCID iDto create a profile. ORCID stands for Open Researcher and Contributor ID. This offers a unique, persistent identifier to all researchers free of charge. If you do not have an ORCID iD you can set one up here.

For registration you will need the following documents:

All documents must be attached electronically in pdf format. If you do not do so you may need to submit your application again.

A photocopy of your passport

This should be your current passport and be in pdf format. Needed at Registration.

A passport sized photo {-#passport-size-photo}

Bahamas/US standard. Minimum 600 pixels by 600 pixels, maximum 1200 by 1200 pixels. The image must be in colour (24 bits per pixel) in sRGB colour space. jpeg or png format
